## Major Character Changes

### Ember - Complete Redesign

**Previous Concept**: Small bioluminescent cave creature
**New Concept**: Humanoid lizard-person warrior

**New Details:**
- **Species**: Lizard-Person (branched from Istars' common ancestor)
- **Appearance**: Humanoid but lizard-like, stands on hind legs regularly
- **Gender**: Female (she/her)
- **Build**: Strong, muscular, combat-focused
- **Clothing**: Light leather armor, hides weapons (unlike Istars who don't wear clothes)

**Combat Abilities:**
- **Primary Weapon**: Spear-sword with ball handle
  - Long spear-like weapon with sword blade
  - Extremely strong grip on ball handle
  - Thrust-focused for tight cave corners
  - Can swing to cut when needed
  - **Special**: Can heat blade through concentration (glows brightly)
- **Secondary Weapon**: Tail blade
  - Long blade attached to tail
  - Can also heat up with concentration
  - Surprise attack capability
- **Expertise**: Fire specialist, cave combat expert

**Personality:**
- More serious than Lee
- Prides herself as fighter
- Deeply respects Gandalf (met on his journeys)
- Gradually warms to Lee's genuine enthusiasm
- Eases up with his attempts at humor

**Key Story Role:**
- Reveals important truth: Creatures like Lee's personality because Gandalf translates his actual thoughts/emotions to them
- They understand Lee better than he understands them
- Should have been obvious but never crossed Lee's mind
- Non-romantic appreciation

### Flubber - New Character

**Species**: Blob (Lee's name)
**Appearance**: Amorphous clear blob, gelatinous, shapeshifting

**Abilities:**
- Shape-shifting at will
- Bioluminescence control (can glow or go dark)
- Creates hard, sharp spikes from body
- Envelops prey
- Paralysis stingers
- Absorbs prey after paralysis
- Perfect camouflage

**Danger Level:**
- One of most dangerous creatures in cave systems
- Can attack from seemingly anywhere
- Nearly undetectable when hiding
- Gandalf warns team about Blobs

**Introduction Scene - The Demon Worm Battle:**

**Setup:**
- Team enters cave with massive demon worm
- Worm has sharp razor teeth, many tentacles
- Incredibly fast and powerful
- Attacking walls seemingly at random
- Gandalf identifies as demon
- Team decides to engage

**Battle:**
1. Everyone attacks at once (except Gandalf who surveys area)
2. Demon faster/stronger than expected
3. Takes damage but shrugs it off
4. Attacks Lee first
5. Gandalf throws rock barrier, protects Lee
6. Gandalf hurls rocks at demon
7. Demon shifts to Gandalf
8. Gandalf slams demon with massive rock
9. Knocks demon back to original position
10. Gandalf pushes demon toward glittering wall

**Flubber's Reveal:**
- Sparkling dark blob appears from nowhere
- Surrounds demon on all sides
- Clutches onto worm's back
- Continuous stabbing
- Demon cannot escape
- Worm falls and disintegrates from back
- Gandalf keeps blob at bay, threatens it

**Conversation:**
- Gandalf asks why they were fighting
- Flubber was fighting demon but losing
- Was hiding from demon
- Thanks team for help
- Asks team's goals
- Discussion leads to alliance
- Flubber joins team

**Role**: Team assassin, stealth specialist, ambush expert

### Ripple - Role Change

**Previous**: Main team member
**New**: Supporting ally, not infiltration team member

**Reason**: Cannot move on land effectively
**New Role**: 
- Ocean-based ally
- Helps team in critical situations
- Indirect support
- Represents ocean societies
- Important but not always present
