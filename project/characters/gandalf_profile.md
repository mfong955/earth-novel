# Gandalf (Istar Mentor)

## Basic Information

**Name**: "Gandalf" (Lee's nickname - Istars don't use names)
**True Identity**: A feeling/projection of multi-layered strata in the earth
**Species**: Istar
**Age**: Thousands of years old (ancient even by Istar standards)
**Role**: Mentor, Explorer, Researcher, First Contact with Lee

---

## Identity & Naming

### Istar Naming Convention
- Istars don't have traditional names
- They project a **feeling** that represents their identity
- This projection changes over time as they grow and develop
- Each Istar's feeling is unique to them

### Gandalf's Projection/Feeling
**Description**: Multi-layered strata in the earth that has been building over geological time scales

**Qualities Conveyed:**
- **Old**: Ancient, experienced, weathered by time
- **Complex**: Made up of many layers, many experiences
- **Strong**: Solid, reliable, enduring
- **Insightful**: Each layer represents knowledge gained
- **Geological**: Connected to deep time and Earth itself
- **Built Over Time**: Accumulated wisdom and power

### Why "Gandalf"
- Lee recognizes similarities to Gandalf from Lord of the Rings
- Wise, powerful, travels and learns
- Patient teacher but can be stern
- Deadly when necessary but enjoys lighter moments
- Lee uses this nickname, which Gandalf accepts with amusement/patience

## Personality

### Core Traits
- **Wise**: Accumulated knowledge over thousands of years
- **Intelligent**: Extremely smart, quick to understand
- **Powerful**: One of the most powerful Istars (but not THE most powerful)
- **Deadly**: Extremely dangerous in combat when needed
- **Patient (Relatively)**: More patient than most Istars, but still impatient by human standards
- **Curious**: Driven to explore and understand
- **Pragmatic**: Knows his limits and works within them

### Behavioral Patterns
- Impatient with humans generally (they're slow to grasp will)
- Finds affinity with Lee specifically
- Can have fun when time allows
- Serious when situation demands
- Balances wisdom with action
- Values genuine, kind, curious minds

### Strengths
- Vast knowledge accumulated over millennia
- Powerful will abilities
- Combat experience
- Understanding of limits (makes him more effective)
- Ability to teach and communicate
- Strategic thinking
- Patience with Lee despite general impatience

### Limitations
- Not as powerful as Prime Evil
- Not the most powerful being besides Prime Evil
- Impatient with most humans
- Limited by Istar understanding of Ancients
- Cannot penetrate Ancient layers without casualties
- Bound by Istar culture and mission

## Background & History

### Early Life
- Born thousands of years ago
- Grew up during time when Ancients were distant mystery
- Developed into powerful Istar over millennia
- Chose path of explorer/researcher

### The Dinosaur Extinction Event
- Witnessed the Ancient's explosion
- Saw hundreds of thousands of Istars die
- Survived the catastrophe
- Shaped by this traumatic event
- Dedicated to understanding Ancients afterward

### Role Among Istars
- One of the powerful explorers/researchers
- Part of elite group with differing goals
- Meets with other powerful Istars every ~10 years
- Shares findings and coordinates efforts
- Respected but not a leader

### Current Mission
- **Primary Goal**: Learn to communicate with surface creatures
- **Secondary Goal**: Gain support to fight Ancient evils
- **Motivation**: Ancient activity increasing in recent decades
- **Method**: Study surface life, attempt first contact
- **Success**: Lee represents breakthrough in human communication
