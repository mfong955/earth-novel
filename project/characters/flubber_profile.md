## Physical Description

### Base Form
- **Appearance**: Amorphous clear blob
- **Consistency**: Gelatinous, shapeshifting
- **Transparency**: Clear/translucent
- **Size**: Variable (can change shape)

### Abilities
- **Shape-Shifting**: Can change form at will
- **Bioluminescence**: Controls own light emission
  - Can glow or go dark
  - Uses for camouflage
- **Spike Formation**: Can create hard, sharp spikes from body
- **Envelopment**: Can surround and engulf prey
- **Stingers**: Creates stingers to paralyze prey
- **Absorption**: Absorbs prey after paralysis

### Camouflage
- Controls bioluminescence to hide
- Changes shape to blend with environment
- Can appear as part of cave wall
- Surprise attack from seemingly anywhere
- Nearly invisible when hiding

## First Encounter: The Demon Worm Battle

### The Scene Setup
**Location**: Cave with massive worm-like demon

**The Demon:**
- Massive worm-like creature
- Sharp razor teeth in circular jaw
- Not smooth - covered in tentacles
- Incredibly fast and powerful
- Attacking walls seemingly at random

**Initial Observation:**
- Team enters cave
- Sees demon attacking walls
- Gandalf identifies it as demon
- Team decides to engage

### The Battle Begins
**Team's Attack:**
- Everyone attacks at once
- Gandalf stands still, surveying with eyes
- Team has initial advantage
- Coordinated assault

**Demon's Response:**
- Faster and stronger than expected
- Takes damage but shrugs it off
- Refocuses on team
- Attacks Lee first

**Gandalf's Protection:**
- Too fast for others to react
- Gandalf throws rock barrier in front of Lee
- Hurls rocks at demon
- Demon shifts attention to Gandalf
- Gandalf slams demon with massive rock
- Knocks demon back to original position

**Gandalf's Strategy:**
- Holds creature off
- Pushes demon toward specific wall
- Wall glitters under Gandalf's light
- Deliberate positioning

### Flubber's Reveal
**The Ambush:**
- Sparkling dark blob appears from nowhere
- Surrounds demon on all sides
- Clutches onto worm's back
- Continuous stabbing
- Demon cannot escape or fight back

**The Kill:**
- Worm falls
- Begins disintegrating from back
- Flubber's absorption in action
- Demon defeated

**Gandalf's Response:**
- Keeps blob at bay
- Threatens death if it approaches
- Maintains control of situation
- Protective of team

### The Conversation

- Asks why they were fighting

**Flubber's Explanation:**
