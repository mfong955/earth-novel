## Underground Locations

### Cave Systems Overview

**Nature:**
- Mostly undiscovered by humans
- Largely unexplored by surface dwellers
- Too difficult for humans to traverse safely
- Vast networks connecting across continents
- Varying depths and dangers

**Istar Mastery:**
- Istars evolved to traverse these efficiently
- Can move quickly through tight spaces
- Navigate in complete darkness
- Know safe routes and dangerous areas
- Gandalf is expert cave traveler

### Upper Cave Networks

**Characteristics:**
- Accessible from surface through hidden entrances
- Some human exploration but limited
- Natural formations: stalactites, stalagmites, underground rivers
- Home to various cave-dwelling creatures
- Relatively safe with Istar guide

**Creatures:**
- Blind cave fish
- Bats (can communicate telepathically)
- Cave insects and arthropods
- Unique underground species
- Most are harmless, some curious about visitors

### Mid-Depth Caverns

**Characteristics:**
- Huge open caverns, cathedral-like spaces
- Bioluminescent creatures providing eerie light
- Underground lakes and rivers
- Strange rock formations
- Temperature changes

**Bioluminescent Life:**
- Glowing fungi on walls
- Luminescent insects
- Phosphorescent bacteria
- Creates otherworldly atmosphere
- Enough light to see by

**Inhabitants:**
- More diverse creature population
- Some intelligent species
- Creatures that respect Gandalf
- Complex minds, doing their own thing
- Gandalf can communicate with most

**Atmosphere:**
- Beautiful but alien
- Sense of hidden world
- Ancient and untouched
- Both wondrous and slightly unsettling

### Istar Territory

**Location:**
- Mid to deep levels
- Above Ancient depths
- Strategic positioning
- Defensible areas

**Characteristics:**
- [To be developed]
- Adapted to Istar needs
- Community spaces
- Research areas
- Meeting places for powerful Istars

### Deep Caverns (Dangerous)

**Characteristics:**
- Even Gandalf doesn't traverse some areas
- Dark things at work
- Unknown to Istars
- Potentially Ancient-controlled
- Extreme danger

**Atmosphere:**
- Oppressive darkness
- Sense of malevolence
- Wrong feeling
- Ancient evil presence
- To be avoided

### Ancient Depths

**Location:**
- Deepest levels of Earth
- Below Istar territory
- Inaccessible without extreme risk
- Where Prime Evil dwells

**Characteristics:**
- Extreme heat and pressure
- Completely dark
- Hostile to surface life
- Ancient evil domain
- Source of abominations

**Istar Knowledge:**
- Cannot penetrate without casualties
- Attempted exploration always fails
- Ancient minds resist Istar will
- Defended by powerful creatures
- Mystery and danger
