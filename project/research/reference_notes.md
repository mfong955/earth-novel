# Research & Reference Notes

## Real-World Inspirations

### Mycelial Networks (Forest Communication)
**Real Science:**
- Trees actually do communicate through underground fungal networks (mycorrhizal networks)
- Dubbed the "Wood Wide Web" by scientists
- Trees share nutrients, water, and chemical signals
- Mother trees nurture younger trees through the network
- Warning signals about pests and diseases travel through the network

**Story Application:**
- Foundation for the forest telepathic network
- Ancient trees as repositories of knowledge
- Fungal networks as physical manifestation of telepathic connections
- Adds scientific credibility to fantasy elements

**Further Research:**
- Suzanne Simard's work on forest ecology
- Peter Wohlleben's "The Hidden Life of Trees"
- Mycorrhizal network studies

### Ocean Intelligence & Communication
**Real Science:**
- Dolphins and whales have complex communication systems
- Octopi show remarkable intelligence despite being solitary
- Many sea creatures use bioluminescence for communication
- Fish schools coordinate through lateral line systems
- Coral reefs are interconnected ecosystems

**Story Application:**
- Ocean societies with their own cultures
- Different communication methods adapted to water
- Struggles with human impact (pollution, noise, overfishing)
- Potential allies with unique perspectives

**Further Research:**
- Cetacean communication studies
- Octopus intelligence research
- Coral reef ecosystem dynamics

### Geological Timescales
**Real Science:**
- Earth is ~4.5 billion years old
- Dinosaur extinction was ~65 million years ago
- Chicxulub impact crater in Gulf of Mexico
- Geological processes work on vast timescales
- Deep Earth conditions are extreme (pressure, heat, darkness)

**Story Application:**
- Ancient evils working on geological timescales
- Gulf of Mexico as site of previous emergence
- Deep Earth as hostile environment requiring adaptation
- Contrast between human and geological time perception

**Further Research:**
- K-Pg extinction event details
- Deep Earth geology
- Impact crater formation

### Telepathy & Consciousness Studies
**Speculative Science:**
- Quantum consciousness theories
- Collective consciousness concepts
- Animal communication beyond sound
- Electromagnetic field sensitivity in animals
- Pheromone communication

**Story Application:**
- Scientific basis for telepathic network
- How telepathy might actually work
- Why humans might have lost this ability
- How it could be regained

**Further Research:**
- Rupert Sheldrake's morphic resonance (controversial)
- Animal magnetoreception

## Notes on Maintaining Consistency

### Keep Track Of:
- Rules of telepathy (range, limitations, learning curve)
- Rules of will power (what it can/can't do, costs, training)
- Timeline of events (how long between major plot points)
- Character locations and movements
- Which characters know what information
- Established facts about the world

### Avoid:
- Contradicting established rules
- Deus ex machina solutions
- Inconsistent character abilities
- Plot holes in the timeline
- Forgetting established world details
